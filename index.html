about

Less is more work

Founded in 2010, Top Chocolate is an Egyptian manufacturer specializing in premium pastry decoration and chocolate accessories. We produce Transfer Sheets, Custom Printed Satin Ribbons, Acrylic Chocolate Molds, Plastic Cake Collars, and a wide range of cake and pastry decoration solutions.

Serving bakeries, confectionery manufacturers, hotels, restaurants, and wholesale distributors across Egypt and international markets, we are committed to delivering superior quality, innovative products, reliable technical support, and fast delivery. Our mission is to help our partners create exceptional desserts with professional finishing solutions.
